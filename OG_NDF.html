Throughout my TDP journey I feel i have been able to acheive the 6 NDF competencies.

NDF Competencies

  • Always Learning
  • Get The job Done
  • Keep Improving
  • Keep Safe
  • Think It Through
  • Work Together

Always Learning

We learn from our experience everyday and take on board feedback.
We keep our skills and knowledge up to date and share with others, using this new thinking to develop and apply in our work

Get The Job Done

With guidance, we plan and manage our own work, prioritising the most important things.
We identify difficulties, monitor and review our progress and work hard to achieve our goals.

Keep Improving

We put forward new and sometimes creative ideas and solutions that are important for out team or community.
We help shape and support change, overcoming any difficulties to embrace news ways of working

Keep Safe

We understand the importance of our standard processes and procedures in keeping us reiable and consistent, and follow these at all times to provide good customer outcomes.
We raise issues and escalate them appropriately.

Think It Through

We look for information to help our understanding, and know where to find missing information.
We find patterns and errors and produce logical and practical solutions to solve in-depth technical and business problems.

Work Together

We actively try to understand other's needs, opinions and expectations and the value of a range of perspectives, giving us a truly inclusive approach.
We look out for the well-being of our colleagues and help them in challenging circumstances.
