CardMS




This was the biggest project i was lucky enough to join. 
It was an end to end process which involved the collaboration of many different teams and colleagues, 
from Architects to Developers.

The objective was to provide a Proof of Concept for the migation of our Token Managment System from Unisys to SAP.
 It was a complete breakdown of the current process and mapping of the functional and non-functional requirements.

This gave me the opportunity to concentrate on a smaller part of Nationwide, systems and processes. 
It was time seeing the effective use of Jira and Confluence to manage the project and document our findings.

I was able to gain first hand experience of the use of Agile and lifecycle of the project by 
attending daily standups and observing how each iteration contributed towards the evolution of the project.

This was a great experience as I gained alot of exposure to the process of project management and collaboration.

I was able to develop my networking and benefit from the use of Jira and Confluence. 
I was able to understand more about the complexity of the infrastructure and architecture 
and start to learn about the systems and processes such Base24, Visa Suite.

I also saw the interactions with 3rd party systems as well as how we 
structure, collect, process data and protect.

Finally, it gave me more opportunity to work with colleagues on the 
SAP System and ABAP language to build towards being able to contribute in my business area.
