TDP Newsletter

										 
"I took part in an interview for the first edition of the TDP Journey Newsletter which 
gave me the chance to talk about my experience of the first 12 weeks of TDP giving insight
on how I felt throughout and what expect for others who want to join the future TDP Cohort.
 
It was also an opportunity to reflect on what I had done over that 12 weeks and how far I had come,
from basically having no experience in computer programming, maybe helping someone down the line who
was going through the same rollercoaster of emotions to help someone gain confidence about taking that
 leap to change careers and try something completely new. 
 
 It was also a chance to network with fellow 
 TDP’ers that I didn’t have a chance to properly meet in the beginning 
(Roua Almakadma and Sakeena Ali)"
