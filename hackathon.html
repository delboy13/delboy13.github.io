Hackathon


	At the end of the 12 week QA Academy crash course was my first hackathon. 
	This was an opportunity to to work in teams and demonstrate what we had learned.
	We had to co-ordinate the design of an application which would connect colleagues with 
	certain	skills they may have or projects they are on and promote collaboration. 

	
	My technical skills were very basic at this point, so my approach
	was to use what I had learned regarding UML and AGILE to support the team. 
	I did this by creating some USE cases for our project to help map & communicate features
	more effectivley taking on the role of product owner & scrum master.
	

	This was great opportunity to gain experience and practice what I had learned and discover
	how it all connected. I was able to implement what i had learned from UML and Agile to contribute
	to the task but more importantly I was able to identify what I needed to work on.
