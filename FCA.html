FCA DATA REQUEST

										 
"One of the first task I was given when I finished the first 12 weeks of TDP and moved into my business area/Team 
was the FCA Data Request Task. 

This involved looking for clarification of data definitions in anticipation of a meeting with the FCA. 
Being honest this was a shock to the system 
and took me completely out of my comfort zone, as it involved having to network around different departments,
contacting colleagues, looking and trying to understand different concepts, processes, policies, 
language used (acronyms) and coming up with results that I was able to contribute back into an answer to help with
the task at hand. 

I came away from that more confused than ever however it helped me get
quickly introduced to team members, departments and systems that I later went on to
study further building on my understanding and realisation on the vast complexity of the business
  I was now involved in. "
